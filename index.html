Project Portfolio of Hushmand Esmaeili

Welcome to my website portfolio! I am a senior/final-year student at Worcester Polytechnic Institute (WPI), where I am pursuing a B.S./M.S. in Robotics Engineering. Throughout my studies, I have been involved in a variety of robotics projects, including those related to robot kinematics and dynamics, robot control, robot manipulation, and motion planning and navigation. My primary interest is in the field of robotics software engineering, specifically in the areas of robot control, multi-body dynamics, optimization, and motion planning. My ultimate goal is to contribute to the advancement of society through the development of robots that can assist and enhance human life. As I am nearing graduation, I am open to job opportunities in the field of robotics.
